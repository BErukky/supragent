# Super Signals Blueprint (v2.0) - High-Precision Analytical Engine

## 1. Core Architecture

Super Signals operates on a 5-layer system designed for maximum objectivity.

---

## 2. Intelligence Layers (v2.0 Upgrade)

### Layer 1: Market Structure & Liquidity

- **Purpose**: Pure structural truth.
- **Upgrade**: Numeric weights for fractal clarity (0-1.0) and sweep magnitude.
- **Constraints**: No indicators, no news, no higher timeframe cross-talk.
- **Confidence Logic**: Clearer HH/HL formations = higher weight. Large wicks into liquidity pools = high interaction score.

### Layer 2: Multi-Timeframe Confluence

- **Purpose**: Coherence check.
- **Upgrade**: **Trend Coherence Score**. Measures the distance and alignment between HTF (e.g., 1h) and LTF (15m).
- **Confidence Logic**: 1.0 = Perfect alignment (Bullish HTF/Bullish LTF). <0.5 = Conflict/Transition.

### Layer 3: Historical Pattern Similarity

- **Purpose**: Humility and statistical context.
- **Upgrade**: **Probabilistic Matching**. Returns a percentage probability based on historical Euclidean distance analogs.
- **Learning**: Tracks recurring false positives in matching (Lowers confidence if a "fake" analog is detected).

### Layer 4: News & Narrative

- **Purpose**: Environmental permission.
- **Upgrade**: **Reaction Assimilation**. Numeric penalties for "contradictory events" (e.g., high-risk hacks during a breakout). Penalizes confidence by fixed weights (-20 to -100).
- **Governance**: Auto-suppress to WAIT if impact > 80 penalty.

### Layer 5: Probability & Risk Governance

- **Aggregation**: Total Confidence = (L1 _ 0.4) + (L2 _ 0.3) + (L3 _ 0.2) + (L4 _ 0.1) \* Governance Modifiers.
- **Risk**: Dynamic SL/TP based on R:R and structure.
- **Feedback Loop**: Every prediction is logged. Future runs check outcomes (at least 5 bars ahead) to calculate "Drift".

---

## 3. Governance Decision Logic (Hard Rules)

- **Wait State**: Mandatory if Confidence < 75 or Layer 4 HIGH risk detected.
- **Accuracy Tracking**: System stores historical results to refine layer weights incrementally.

---

**Instruction to Agent: Preserve structural truth above all. Confidence is earned, not assumed.**
